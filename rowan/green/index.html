[−][src]Module rowan::green

The "green tree" is the immutable, atomically reference counted tree.

Leaf tokens are GreenToken and non-leaf nodes are GreenNode. Both of these types are unsized and are only used behind references. All tree nodes are aggressively reference counted and reused, to minimize space taken up by the immutable tree and to maximize sharing.
